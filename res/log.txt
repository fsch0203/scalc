Promotion text
Mouseless Stack-Calculator is a high precision scientific calculator and currency converter with fast keyboard entry, with stack-mode and RPN-mode. It supports several notations including fractions and complex numbers. <br>
Main features:<br>
<ul>
<li>Flexible and intuitive user interface</li>
<li>Complete insight in entered expression</li>
<li>All the advantages of RPN in RPN-mode</li>
<li>Keyboard for all data entry (as you would expect from a calculator)</li>
<li>Most frequent used instructions only need 1 key; other instructions 2 keys</li>
<li>Integrated currency converter with actual rates (from ECB or Yahoo)</li>
<li>Arbitrary-precision decimal representation (no rounding errors)</li>
<li>Multi-level undo</li>
<li>Support for complex numbers and polar/rectangular conversions</li>
<li>Flexible number notation: normal, financial, scientific, engineering, h:m:s, h:m, hexadecimal, octal, binary, fractions, complex (rectangular and polar)</li>
<li>30 last answers</li>
<li>30 memories to store intermediate results</li>
<li>Stack and memory are saved on your computer for future use</li>
<li>Copy results to clipboard for use in other programs</li>
<li>Mathematical calculations</li>
<li>Works with hours (or degrees), minutes, seconds</li>
<li>Statistical and probability calculations</li>
<li>Financial calculations like CAGR, Annuity and CashFlow</li>
<li>Physical constants and conversions</li>
</ul>
